About the Story

In a world where the sky sometimes forgets its lullabies, twelve-year-old Elias draws maps of places that have never been, and eleven-year-old Mara tends lanterns that remember every dream ever dreamed.

When the last true star begins to fade, they board a ship with sails woven from glass and moonlight. Together they must sail beyond the edge of every charted sea to find the Song that holds the heavens together — before silence falls forever.
