About the Book

When the stars begin to forget their own songs, a quiet boy who draws maps of places that don’t yet exist and a gentle girl who keeps lanterns that remember dreams set sail on a ship with sails made of glass and ribs of whale-bone. Together, Elias and Mara must find the lost lully of the sky before the last lullaby fades forever.

A heart-warming, lyrical fantasy for ages 8–12 (and every grown-up who still believes in starlight).

Elias

The young cartographer who maps dreams and carries ink on his cheeks like freckles.

Mara

The lantern-keeper whose glowing light guides lost lullabies home.
